About the Digestive System

The digestive system breaks down food into nutrients that can be absorbed by the body. It's a complex process involving multiple organs working together.

Key Concepts:

  • Mechanical Digestion: Physical breakdown of food
  • Chemical Digestion: Enzymatic breakdown of food
  • Absorption: Nutrient uptake into the bloodstream
  • Elimination: Removal of waste products

Main Organs:

  • Mouth: Mechanical and chemical digestion begins
  • Esophagus: Transports food to stomach
  • Stomach: Breaks down food with acids and enzymes
  • Small Intestine: Nutrient absorption
  • Large Intestine: Water absorption and waste formation
