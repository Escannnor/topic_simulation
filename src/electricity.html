Understanding Electricity

Electricity is the flow of electric charge through a conductor. This simulation helps you understand the fundamental concepts of electricity and electrical circuits.

What is Electricity?

Electricity is a form of energy resulting from the existence of charged particles (such as electrons or protons), either statically as an accumulation of charge or dynamically as a current.

How do Electrical Circuits Work?

An electrical circuit is a path through which electric current flows. It consists of various components like resistors, capacitors, and power sources connected by conductive wires.

Real-world Applications

Understanding electricity is crucial in many fields:

  • Power generation and distribution
  • Electronics and computing
  • Electric vehicles
  • Renewable energy systems
