About the Human Heart

The heart is a muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body, delivering oxygen and nutrients to tissues and removing waste products.

Key Concepts:

  • Cardiac Cycle: The heart's pumping action
  • Blood Flow: How blood moves through the heart
  • Heart Chambers: Atria and ventricles
  • Heart Valves: Control blood flow direction

Main Parts of the Heart:

  • Right Atrium: Receives deoxygenated blood
  • Right Ventricle: Pumps blood to lungs
  • Left Atrium: Receives oxygenated blood
  • Left Ventricle: Pumps blood to body
  • Valves: Prevent backflow of blood
  • Coronary Arteries: Supply heart muscle
