About Light and Optics

This simulation demonstrates the principles of light refraction and dispersion through different media. You can observe how light waves change direction and separate into different colors when passing through a prism.

Key Concepts:

  • Light refraction and Snell's law
  • Dispersion of light
  • Wavelength and color
